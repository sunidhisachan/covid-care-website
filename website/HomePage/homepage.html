Things not to do during Covid.

In this situation, every one of us should take all the measures possible to avoid an infection.The number of people infected by COVID-19 is increasing. This can be avoided if people avoid doing the following things.

  • Avoid Stepping Out Without a Face Cover.
  • Don’t Ignore Government-issued Advice.
  • Avoid Assuming That You are Immune to COVID-19.
  • Avoid Touching Your Face.
  • Don’t Touch Open Surfaces in Public Places.

Things to do during Covid.

In this situation, every one of us should take all the measures possible to avoid an infection.The number of people infected by COVID-19 is increasing. This can be avoided if people do the following things.

  • Practice Social Distancing.
  • Seek Medical Help if Symptoms Occur.
  • Avoid Going Out if it's not unnecessary.
  • Maintain the 6-foot distance rule.
  • Wash your hands for 20 sec.
